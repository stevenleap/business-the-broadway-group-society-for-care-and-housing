About · Field Notes

The Broadway Group Society for Care and Housing is a nonprofit based in Vancouver, BC, dedicated to providing care and housing to our community. Located at 245 Powell St, we have been a steady presence in the Downtown Eastside, offering programs that support dignity and stability.

  • Rooted in Vancouver's Downtown Eastside for years.
  • Focus on both care and housing as interconnected needs.
  • Community-driven approach with local volunteers.

Service area: Vancouver's Downtown Eastside and surrounding areas.
